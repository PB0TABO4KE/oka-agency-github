About us

The OKA Agency helps foreign citizens make the move to Russia as easy as possible. We assist in obtaining TRPs, residence permits, and Russian citizenship as well as also providing personal consultations.

Our team of professional lawyers are ready to help you obtain the required documents and settle into your new life in Russia.

Services

  • A temporary residence permit in the Russian Federation (TRP)
    A document that provides an opportunity for a foreign citizen to legally stay and work in the Russian Federation from 90 days to 3 years
    Read more
  • A residence permit
    A document that gives a foreigner the right to permanently live in Russia, freely enter and exit it, work legally and enjoy the fundamental rights of a citizen
    Read more
  • Citizenship of the Russian Federation
    Российское гражданство позволяет пользоваться всеми правами россиянина, в том числе голосовать на выборах, работать в правоохранительных органах, занимать государственные должности
    Read more
  • Simplified citizenship of the Russian Federation
    In the legislation of the Russian Federation there is a simplified (accelerated) procedure for obtaining Russian citizenship for certain categories of foreigners
    Read more
  • Compatriot resettlement program
    A program for the resettlement of people who found themselves outside of Russia after the collapse of the USSR and wishing to return to their historical homeland.
    Read more
  • Customer service
    Help and advice on an extended list of issues that a foreigner moving to Russia may encounter.
