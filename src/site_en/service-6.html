Customer service

Customer service

Providing assistance and advice on an expanded list of issues that a foreigner moving to the Russian Federation may face. Including:

  1. 01Search and assistance in acquiring movable and immovable property.
  2. 02Search and help in hiring service personnel (nannies, housekeepers, drivers, etc.).
  3. 03Assistance in enrolling children in preschool and school educational institutions.
  4. 04Documentary support for opening a legal entity.


In addition, we provide full documentary support to our clients already living in the Russian Federation- timely notification of government services within the framework of migration legislation helps to maintain their acquired status in the Russian Federation.
